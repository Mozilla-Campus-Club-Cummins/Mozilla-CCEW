About Us

The Mozilla Campus Club of CCEW was formed to promote the idea of open source among aspiring students and encourage them to make the most of the opportunities that come along with it. Various events have been conducted along with staying connected via social media platforms and running a blog on open-source development. Over the years, we have successfully conducted workshops related to GitHub, and webinars with notable speakers ranging from web development and artificial intelligence to many more alongside the demonstrative lectures conducted. Furthermore, the club provides a platform to participate in prominent competitions like Hacktoberfest, GSoC, etc.

Mission

To cultivate a vibrant community of students passionate about open source

Vision

To create a dynamic culture of creativity and collaborative learning towards open source technology.

Values

We foster an inclusive environment that values continuous learning, innovation, transparency and community engagement.
